The Infamous Taco Salad

Description

This is a recipe that I stumbled upon while browsing and I just had to share it with you guys. Delicious, easy and most of all : healthy !

Ingredients

For 4 servings

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) taco seasoning mix
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 6 cups Romaine or Iceberg lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese or cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup guacamole
  • 1 cup Fritos or tortilla chips
  • OPTIONAL TOPPINGS:
  • pico de gallo, salsa, olives, cilantro, etc.

Preparation steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the beef over medium high heat. Drain. Add the taco seasoning and water and cook for 2-3 minutes or until the liquid reduces. Let slightly cool.
  2. Assemble salads by placing the lettuce in a bowl and adding the meat, tomatoes, cheese, onion, sour cream, guacamole, and chips. Add other desired toppings and serve!
